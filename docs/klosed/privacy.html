Information Collection and Use

The App allows you:

  • To throw parties or other kinds of events (the “Events”)./li>
  • To join to Events near you thrown by the other users of the App.

For a better experience while using the App, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information about you (your “Profile Data”), including but not limited to:

  • Name
  • Birth date
  • E-mail address
  • Address, State, Province, ZIP/Postal code, City, and the corresponding geographic coordinates.

The information that we request will be retained by us on our servers and used by the App to share your Profile Data with the other users of the App.
You can choose to restrict the sharing of your Profile Data, but the App will share them anyway with:

  • The hosts of the Events to which you have asked to join.
  • The guests of your own Events.

Real time location information of the device

The App can collect precise information about the location of your mobile device in order:

  • To convert your geographic location to an address that may be the addresscof an Event you are organizing.
  • To show you on a map the Events near to your geographic location.

The location of your mobile device is retained on your device and is not collected by us in any way.
You can choose to not allow the App to access the location of your mobile device, but you will not be able to use the features based on that.

Photos, media and files on your device

The App can ask for accessing photos, media and files on your device in order:

  • To attach an avatar to your Profile Data.
  • To attach a photo and/or a video to your Events.

You can choose to not allow the App to access photos, media and files on your device, but you will not be able to use the features based on that.

Camera

The App can ask for using the camera embedded in your device in order:

  • To scan the QR code corresponding to the virtual ticket of a guest to enter an Event of yours.

The acquired images are retained on your device and are not collect by us in any way.
You can choose to not allow the App to use the embedded camera of your device, but you will not be able to use the features based on that.
